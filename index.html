I worked at Equatorial Energies as an intern solar technician. I was involved in installation of solar PV systems at different field sites, and in monitoring and O&M. I also helped provide consultancy services to clients wishing to integrate solar with their energy systems.

At Kenya Power, I served in the E-Plant department where working among a team of technicians I was involved in maintenance of power and distribution transformers and in managing SCADA control panels at the power substation.
